Pre-Primary Education

Nursery, LKG & UKG (Ages 2.5 - 5 years)

Building strong foundations through play-based learning, curiosity, and early childhood development.

Our Pre-Primary Philosophy

Every parent appreciates that these early years in education are a time of curiosity, discovery, and learning. Our Preschool curriculum is inquiry and play-based, with a strong focus on developing early Literacy and Numeracy skills.

Great emphasis is placed on developing early reading and writing skills, so that each child is given a "head start" in their education. The wonderful world of stories, nursery rhymes, songs, role-play, and drama within a language-rich learning environment stimulates children's curiosity.

Program Highlights

  • Hands-on, fun, and exciting curriculum designed by EZ Vidya Chrysalis
  • Play-based learning with structured activities
  • Development of communication and language skills
  • Focus on gross and fine motor skills
  • Building positive self-sense and relationships

Design Thinking Playbook

Our comprehensive guide and roadmap for implementing Stanford-style design thinking from Nursery to Grade XII

Stage 1: Empathize

Understanding users through observation, interviews, and immersion

  • User interviews & shadowing
  • Empathy mapping exercises
  • Journey mapping workshops

Stage 2: Define

Framing the problem and identifying key insights

  • Point of view (POV) statements
  • "How Might We" questions
  • Problem definition frameworks

Stage 3: Ideate

Generating creative solutions through brainstorming

  • Brainstorming sessions
  • SCAMPER technique
  • Mind mapping & sketching

Stage 4: Prototype

Building quick, low-fidelity versions to explore ideas

  • Paper prototypes & wireframes
  • 3D printing & maker labs
  • Digital mockups & simulations

Stage 5: Test

Gathering feedback and iterating on solutions

  • User testing sessions
  • Feedback collection & analysis
  • Iterative refinement cycles
  • Final presentation & showcase
